# English Proficiency Test Requirement

## Overview
This page explains the English proficiency standards required for international applicants to BYU-Idaho, including minimum test scores, accepted testing formats, guidance for improving English skills, and circumstances in which a waiver may be considered. Students should review this information before submitting their admissions application.

---

## Essential Information
Required Scores on Proficiency Tests  
Prospective students taking an English proficiency test must meet the minimum required score for the individual subject tests AND the overall composite score for the exam. If English is your second language, you must meet the following English proficiency requirements (TOEFL, IELTS, PTE, or Cambridge English).

### Required Minimum Scores

| Subject Area / Test | TOEFL | IELTS | PTE | Cambridge English |
|---------------------|-------|-------|-----|-------------------|
| Reading             | 17    | 6.0   | 52  | 169               |
| Writing             | 17    | 5.0   | 52  | 154               |
| Listening           | 16    | 6.0   | 52  | 169               |
| Speaking            | 16    | 5.5   | 52  | 162               |
| Use of English      | Not Applicable | Not Applicable | Not Applicable | 169 |
| Composite           | 79    | 6.5   | 52  | 169               |

**TOEFL**  
The TOEFL iBT is the Test of English as a Foreign Language Internet-based test. The TOEFL exam is administered by the Educational Testing Service numerous times each year throughout the world. BYU-Idaho only accepts the TOEFL iBT, TOEFL iBT Home Edition, and TOEFL iBT Paper Edition. Scores from the TOEFL Essentials, TOEFL PBT (paper-based test), and other versions will not be considered.

**IELTS**  
The IELTS is the International English Language Testing System and is administered numerous times each year throughout the world. For more information about testing times and locations, visit the IELTS page.

**PTE**  
The PTE is the Pearson Test of English. Applicants who choose this exam must take the PTE Academic Exam, not the general Pearson Test of English. You may obtain more information on the Pearson page.

**Cambridge English Qualifications**  
Cambridge English Tests are produced by the IELTS company. BYU-Idaho will only accept scores from the C1 Advanced, C2 Proficiency, and B2 First exams.

**English Language Help**  
BYU-Idaho does not offer an ESL (English as a Second Language) program. Students who do not meet the required proficiency scores are encouraged to continue developing their English skills and retest. The English Language Center at Brigham Young University may also be able to help you improve your skills. Learn more at <http://elc.byu.edu>.

**English Proficiency Waiver Requests**  
BYU-Idaho rarely grants English proficiency waivers. These waivers are intended for applicants who have demonstrated readiness for academic work in English. Only applicants who meet one of the following criteria will be reviewed:

1. Successful completion of 24 or more credits in the past two years on campus at an accredited U.S. college or university with a minimum cumulative GPA of 3.0. (PathwayConnect credit will not count toward the 24 credits.)
2. Completion of all years of high school in the U.S.

Waiver requests can be submitted to `admprocessing@byui.edu`. Official transcripts verifying college or high school academics must be sent to `transcriptsubmit@byui.edu` and processed before the waiver request is reviewed. Waiver requests are reviewed by a committee, and approval is not guaranteed.
